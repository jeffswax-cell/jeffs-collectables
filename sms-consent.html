Program Description

Jeff's Digital Collectibles LLC offers an optional SMS-based enquiry service that lets potential buyers contact Jeff directly about digital collectible items listed on this website. SMS is one of two ways to enquire — you can also email jeffswax@gmail.com at any time without opting in to SMS. When you choose to use the SMS opt-in form above or the SMS option on the "Enquire" button on any item, you can send a text message to Jeff about that specific item.

How to Opt In

SMS is optional — you can always enquire by email at jeffswax@gmail.com. If you choose SMS, you opt in by completing the form above or the enquiry form on the homepage. To opt in to SMS, you:

  • Enter your phone number
  • Check the consent checkbox confirming you agree to receive SMS messages from Jeff's Digital Collectibles LLC
  • Click "Send Enquiry via SMS" to proceed

Your consent is specific to the item you're enquiring about. Each new enquiry requires a separate opt-in.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You'll receive SMS messages only in direct response to your enquiry about a specific item. Typical conversations involve 2-10 messages while discussing availability, pricing, and transfer details for the item you asked about. We will not send you promotional messages, marketing texts, or messages about items you didn't enquire about.

Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Jeff's Digital Collectibles LLC does not charge any fees for SMS messages. Contact your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan.

How to Opt Out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message from us
  • Sending the word STOP to (715) 894-6330
  • Emailing jeffswax@gmail.com with your phone number and a request to stop messages

After opting out, you'll receive one final confirmation message. You will not receive any further SMS messages from us unless you initiate a new enquiry and opt in again.

Help

For help with SMS messages, you can:

  • Reply HELP to any message from us for assistance
  • Email jeffswax@gmail.com
  • Call (715) 894-6330

Supported Carriers

SMS messages are sent via standard SMS/MMS protocols and should work with all major US mobile carriers, including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and their subsidiaries. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Your phone number and the content of our SMS conversations are kept private. We will not share, sell, or rent your phone number or message content to any third party. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Terms

By opting in to SMS messages, you also agree to our Terms of Service. SMS consent is not a condition of purchase. You can always contact us via email at jeffswax@gmail.com instead of SMS.
